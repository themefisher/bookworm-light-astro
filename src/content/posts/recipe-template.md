These easy refried kidney beans are everything you want in a side dish — creamy, deeply savory, and ready in under 30 minutes. They're made with pantry staples you probably already have and require almost no effort.

Whether you're stuffing them into tacos, layering them in burritos, or just scooping them up with chips, this recipe never disappoints.

## Why You'll Love This Recipe

- **Ready in 30 minutes** — from pantry to plate, no soaking required
- **5 simple ingredients** — garlic, olive oil, cumin, beans, and lime
- **Naturally vegan and gluten-free** — works for almost any diet
- **Better than store-bought** — richer, fresher, and fully customizable

## Key Ingredients

**Kidney beans** are the star here. Their firm texture holds up beautifully when mashed, giving you that thick, creamy consistency. Canned works perfectly — just rinse them well.

**Garlic and cumin** are the flavor backbone. Don't skip the cumin — it's what makes refried beans taste like refried beans.

**Lime juice** brightens everything up at the end and balances the earthy flavors.

## Easy Substitutions & Variations

- Swap kidney beans for **black beans or pinto beans** for a different flavor profile
- Use **chicken broth** instead of vegetable broth for extra richness (no longer vegan)
- Add **chipotle peppers in adobo** for a smoky, spicy kick
- Stir in **cream cheese** for an ultra-creamy, indulgent version

## Recipe Notes & Tips

Start with a **hot pan** before adding the garlic — it should sizzle immediately. This gives you that light golden color without burning.

Don't mash the beans completely dry. Leave them a little loose in the pan; they firm up as they cool.
