---
question: "What is included in a STORY in-person event?"
category: "In-Person"
order: 8
---

Each in-person STORY event will begin with a 30-minute information session that shares general details about the college admissions and financial aid application processes. After the information session, 25-30 institutions will offer a college fair for 60-90 minutes. Each school will have a table with an admissions representative present to answer your questions about their institution.
