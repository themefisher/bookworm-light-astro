---
question: "What is the difference between STORY and STARS?"
category: "STORY"
order: 2
---

The STORY Consortium and the Small Town and Rural Students (STARS) College Network share a similar goal of increasing access to higher education for students from rural and small-town communities. Both groups host in-person and virtual events to carry out this mission. STORY institutions may change from year to year or between in-person stops, and they do not receive funding related to STORY. STARS members are a set group of institutions funded by the Trott Family Philanthropies for rural-based initiatives.
