---
question: "Who can attend STORY events?"
category: "In-Person"
order: 11
---

STORY is intended for anyone interested in learning more about the college admissions process in general. High school students may find it the most useful, but we encourage parents, caregivers, teachers, school administrators or school counselors to attend as well. Younger siblings or other interested students who wish to learn more about the colleges and universities in attendance are also welcome!
