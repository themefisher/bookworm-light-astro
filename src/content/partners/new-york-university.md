Great cities are engines of creativity, and New York University takes its name and spirit from one of the most diverse and dynamic cities of all. The university lives within New York and other great cities around the world — from Abu Dhabi to Shanghai, Paris to Prague, Sydney to Buenos Aires — as a home for earth’s boldest thinkers, creators, and doers. At NYU, culture is as important as classroom, and the free exchange of ideas bridges societies, borders, and time zones. At NYU, the drive to learn, connect, and understand creates the foundation for a future of community building and innovation. So at any moment, at any time of day, you can always find NYU students, professors, staff, and alumni working hard to make this world a better place.
