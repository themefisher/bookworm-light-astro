Notre Dame is a national Catholic research university dedicated to fostering a more just and humane world through education, research, and service. Founded in 1842, it is ranked among the top 25 national institutions of higher learning. Here you will find the resources and support to explore your passions, develop your talents, and live with purpose. Students work alongside leading experts and turn ideas into action through research, internships and creative projects that extend beyond the classroom. You will join an international community of peers, faculty, and alumni who celebrate innovation and uphold the lifelong bonds of the Notre Dame family.
