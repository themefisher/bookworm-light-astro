At Ohio State, you’ll find opportunity on a scale few others can match. Whether you begin on the Columbus campus or at one of our five regional campuses in Lima, Mansfield, Marion, Newark or Wooster, you’ll be part of the #1 university in Ohio with unparalleled access to transformational experiences, including opportunities in research, study abroad, and student organizations.
