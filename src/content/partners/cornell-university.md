At Cornell University, students can explore their interests and grow while being supported by a diverse and welcoming community. The atmosphere on campus is energetic and collaborative, offering a mix of challenging classes, lots of student activities, and a strong focus on doing the greatest good. Cornellians get to connect with others while diving into academic and real-world issues, with plenty of opportunities to learn, lead, and have fun along the way. Cornell's main campus is in Ithaca, NY. Nestled in New York's Finger Lake region, Ithaca is a unique college town that is consistently ranked as one of the most beautiful in the nation.
