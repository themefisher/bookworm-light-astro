The College of the Holy Cross is a small, liberal arts, undergraduate only, Catholic, Jesuit institution. To put it simply, we are a small community that values the idea of our students broadening their horizons and preparing them for life after college. Our community wants to see everyone pursue their passions, explore newfound interests, and ultimately succeed. Holy Cross is dedicated to helping students bring what they learn in the classroom and apply it in real-world contexts. The student body is committed to making everyone feel that they belong – There is something for everyone on our campus, and there’s no shortage of student led programs. Holy Cross is located in Worcester, the second largest city in New England, which has much to offer its residents. Holy Cross is committed to creating relationships between students and the surrounding Worcester community.
