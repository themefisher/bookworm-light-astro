Williams College is located on Mohican homelands in the region of Massachusetts now known as the Berkshires. Williams offers unique academic opportunities like our renowned tutorial classes (just two students and one professor!) and robust research in every discipline. Our 2,200 students have access to 65 areas of study; nearly 50% major in STEM and 40% double major. Williams meets 100% of demonstrated need and is home to the nation's first All-Grant Financial Aid program, which means NO loans and NO work requirement, in addition to FREE textbooks, health insurance, summer storage and more for all students on aid.
