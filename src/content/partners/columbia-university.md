Columbia has been a place of deep intellectual curiosity and academic inquiry since 1754. Across departments, schools, centers and institutes, Columbians are driven by a passion for discovery, working in the service of improving human understanding and the advancement of our global society. 6,700+ undergrads come from a dazzling array of ethnic, social, economic, cultural, religious and geographic backgrounds, including more than 140 countries. Located in one of the world's most international cities, Columbia University offers an exceptional foundation in the liberal arts, sciences, and engineering across more than 100 areas of study. Easy access to the cultural, professional and recreational resources of New York City influences many aspects of life on our residential campus.
