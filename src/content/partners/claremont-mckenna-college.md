Claremont McKenna College is a liberal arts college located in Claremont, California, less than an hour east of the city of Los Angeles. We’re a member of the Claremont Colleges consortium which consists of five undergraduate liberal arts colleges and two graduate schools. CMC has just under 1,400 students, and was founded in 1946 with a mission to prepare our students for thoughtful and productive lives and responsible leadership in business, government, and the professions. We focus on learning for the sake of doing, leadership skills development, and public policy understanding, which sets our students up for success as leaders in their respective careers.
