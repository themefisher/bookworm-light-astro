The University of Michigan has been leading, driving, and changing the world for over 200 years. At U-M you’ll learn and live alongside students from all 50 states and 100+ countries in Ann Arbor — the No. 2 Best College Town in America (WalletHub). Experience the best of both worlds with everything a large public university offers paired with endless opportunities to make U-M feel small: 1,700+ student organizations, cultural and identity-based groups, and academic and well-being support resources. Wherever Wolverines go, progress follows. Don’t just take our word for it: Money.com rates U-M as one of its best colleges for quality, affordability, and student outcomes, while U.S. News & World Report ranks U-M as the No. 3 undergraduate public university in the U.S.
