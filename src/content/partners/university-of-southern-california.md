The University of Southern California is a large private research university located two miles from Downtown LA, on a beautiful, park-like campus. Academically, small class sizes and plenty of interdisciplinary opportunities allow students to explore their passions and find new ones. Students enjoy a vibrant campus culture with abundant school spirit, and get to take advantage of the endless cultural, entertainment, and internship opportunities that exist in the country’s second largest city. At USC, students become more than just classmates and alumni – they join the Trojan Family, becoming part of a lifelong and worldwide community of fellow Trojans.
