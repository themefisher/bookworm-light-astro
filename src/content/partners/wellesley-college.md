Wellesley is a place filled with changemakers, barrier breakers, and bridge builders. Wellesley is a liberal arts college and women’s college that cheers on the boldest ideas and celebrates the brightest solutions. Our community is one of belonging. 

Wellesley is located 12 miles outside of Boston with 2,300 undergraduates (and only undergraduates!) from around the US and the world. Wellesley is financially possible: we meet 100% of the calculated need of every student with an average financial aid package of over $66,000. Wellesley is a place where students actively explore who they are and who they might become. With 56 majors and minors, 180 student organizations, perhaps too many traditions, and research and internship opportunities that start as early as your first year, the possibilities truly are endless.
