The California Institute of Technology (Caltech) is located in Pasadena, CA, and marshals some of the world’s brightest minds and most innovative tools to address fundamental scientific questions and pressing societal challenges. We are a small, collaborative community defined by our passion for STEM. Caltech has one of the nation’s lowest student-to-faculty ratios (3:1), with 300 faculty members, and the approximately 1,000 undergraduates have access to hands-on research as early as their first year.

While Caltech students all share a common passion for STEM, they go all-in on their passions beyond the laboratory as well, through nine different residential communities, over 100 different student clubs, and 16 NCAA Division III teams.
