A liberal arts College in the midst of a world-renowned research university, UChicago offers a dynamic and well-rounded education, one where student engage with great thinkers, sharpen critical thinking skills, and engage in the Life of the Mind. We’re intent on changing the world by asking tough questions, celebrating new ideas, and forming a community of scholars—one as supportive as it is transformative. The University of Chicago is deeply committed to ensuring that students from every background, regardless of financial need, can find a home which is why we meet 100% of demonstrated need loan-free.
