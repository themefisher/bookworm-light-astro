Smith is a world-class, women’s liberal arts and sciences college. We offer over 50 majors, including engineering, and our open curriculum gives you the freedom to explore. We believe in the
importance of affordability. We meet full demonstrated need with no loans, and you’ll have access to fellowships, internship funding, and guaranteed housing. Smithies hail from all over the country (nearly 50 states), and the world. Our community is unlike any other; it is built of peers, faculty, alums - all empowering your dreams. Smithies elevate and celebrate each other. With our undergraduate focus, you’re our faculty's top priority in classes and research. After four years, you will graduate a more powerful version of yourself, confident in your ability to make an impact in the world.
