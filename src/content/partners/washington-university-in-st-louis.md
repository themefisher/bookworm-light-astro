WashU as a community is committed to working to serve St. Louis, the United States, and the world through innovation and collaboration in politics, medicine, business, technology, and the arts. While there is no one type of WashU student, our students have many things in common. They are collaborative, intellectually curious, creative, and demonstrate leadership in a variety of ways. They share concern for others, seek solutions, and support one another. We also know that our classrooms, residence halls, and common spaces are enriched by being a community of people with a broad array of experiences, backgrounds, perspectives, and interests.
