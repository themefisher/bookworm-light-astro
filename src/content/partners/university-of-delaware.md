The University of Delaware is an R1 public research university with land, sea, and space grants in a vibrant Mid‑Atlantic college town. With under 18,000 undergraduates, UD combines a close-knit community with big‑school spirit. U.S. News ranks UD #86 among national universities. Its chemical engineering undergraduate program sits #4, with nursing at #74. Students can join over 400 clubs, compete or cheer at Division I games, and participate in Greek life, which engages a quarter of the campus. UD is a hub of innovation and service, with research and outreach that impact local communities and industries. The campus buzzes with world-class speakers, arts performances, and a passionate Blue Hen spirit—all enriching a strong academic foundation.
