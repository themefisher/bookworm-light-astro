At Johns Hopkins University, intellectual curiosity and the exchange of diverse perspectives shape every aspect of the undergraduate experience. Guided by a mission to create knowledge for the world, Hopkins offers the resources and opportunities of the nation’s first and top-funded research university, paired with the support of a close-knit undergraduate community. Students are deeply engaged in their fields, working alongside renowned faculty on groundbreaking research and creative projects. Outside the classroom, our residential campus fosters a vibrant, collaborative environment through a wide range of student organizations, cultural events, and campus traditions. Here, students don’t just study — they live, learn, and grow together, developing as leaders and innovators who make a meaningful impact on the world.
