Georgia Tech’ s community reflects its mission: developing leaders who advance technology and improve the human condition. Nestled in Atlanta’s midtown district, our 400-acre campus fosters a welcoming environment for research, entrepreneurship, collaboration, and hands-on learning. Atlanta serves as an economic hub, generating a wealth of opportunities for real-time work and career exploration, a vibrant cityscape, as well as a living laboratory for students to explore. Georgia Tech’s community is committed to cultivating positive global impact while preparing future leaders in technology and innovation. Georgia Tech is a place where ambition meets opportunity.
